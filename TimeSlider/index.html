Timezone Slider

Privacy Policy

This app is built to be simple and respectful of your privacy. Here is what it does and does not collect, and how it handles your data.

1. What the app collects

The app does not collect personal information about you and does not try to identify who you are.

To understand crashes and general feature usage, the app uses two trusted third party services.

Sentry

Used to capture anonymized crash reports. This helps find bugs and improve stability. Crash reports may include device model and iOS version but never include calendar data or anything personal.

TelemetryDeck

Used to understand how the app is being used in a broad, anonymous way. This helps guide improvements. TelemetryDeck does not track you across apps and does not create user profiles.

All data sent to these services is anonymous.

2. No accounts and no sign ups

The app does not require an account or any personal information to use it.

3. Permissions

The app requests access only to features needed for its core functionality.

Calendar

Requested so the app can create events in your Apple Calendar. It only writes events when you choose to create one. It does not read your existing calendars and does not send calendar data anywhere.

4. Subscriptions

The app uses standard Apple StoreKit for subscriptions. Payments, renewals, refunds, and security are all handled by Apple.

The app receives only the information Apple provides to developers, such as whether a subscription is active. It does not receive your name, email, or payment details.

5. No ads or tracking for advertising

The app does not run ads. No data is collected or shared for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. How data is handled

No data is sold or shared with advertisers or third parties outside of Sentry and TelemetryDeck, which handle anonymized diagnostics and usage signals as described above.

7. Children

The app is not designed for children and does not knowingly collect information that could identify a child.

8. Contact

Questions or concerns about privacy can be sent to verysimplefeedback@gmail.com. Feedback is always welcome.

Summary

  • No personal profile is created.
  • Crash data through Sentry is anonymized.
  • Usage data through TelemetryDeck is anonymous.
  • Calendar access is used only to create events you choose.
  • Subscriptions are handled securely by Apple StoreKit.
  • No ads. No selling of data.

The app keeps things simple, transparent, and respectful of your privacy.
