Timezone Slider

Privacy Policy

This app is built to be simple and respectful of your privacy. Here is what the app collects, what it does not collect, and how it handles your data.

1. What the app collects

The app does not collect personal information about you and does not try to identify who you are.

To understand crashes and general usage patterns, the app uses two third party services.

Sentry

Sentry is used to capture anonymized crash reports. This helps find bugs and improve stability. Crash reports may include device model, iOS version, and the point where the app failed. They do not include calendar data, personal information, or anything sensitive.

TelemetryDeck

The app uses TelemetryDeck to understand how features are used in a broad and anonymous way. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal information. It does not store names, emails, IP addresses, device identifiers, or location data.

Before any analytics data leaves your device, TelemetryDeck applies a salted cryptographic hash. This makes the data anonymous and prevents anyone, including TelemetryDeck, from identifying individual users. TelemetryDeck is based in the European Union and is fully compliant with GDPR.

The app sends high level usage signals such as app opens, number of time zones added, time format preference, onboarding status, feature usage, and whether the app is in the free or pro state. The data is used only to understand general trends and improve the app.

2. No accounts and no sign ups

The app does not require an account and does not ask for personal details.

3. Permissions

The app requests access only to features needed for its main functionality.

Calendar

Calendar access is used to create events in your Apple Calendar when you choose to do so. The app does not read your existing calendar events and does not send calendar data anywhere.

4. Subscriptions

The app uses standard Apple StoreKit for subscriptions and in app purchases.

All payments, renewals, receipts, and refunds are handled by Apple. Your payment information is never visible to the app.

The app only receives the information Apple provides to developers, such as whether a subscription is active. It does not receive your name, email address, or any payment details.

5. No ads or marketing tracking

The app does not run ads and does not collect or share data for advertising or marketing.

6. How data is handled

No data is sold or shared with advertisers.

Crash data and anonymous usage signals are processed only by Sentry and TelemetryDeck as described above.

7. Children

The app is not designed for children and does not knowingly collect information that could identify a child.

8. Contact

Questions or concerns about privacy can be sent via email. Feedback is welcome.

Summary

  • No personal profile is created
  • Sentry handles anonymized crash reports
  • TelemetryDeck handles anonymous usage analytics and does not identify users
  • Calendar access is used only when you ask the app to create an event
  • Subscriptions are handled securely by Apple StoreKit
  • No ads and no selling of data

The app aims to stay simple, transparent, and respectful of your privacy.
