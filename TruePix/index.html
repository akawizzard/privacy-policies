Privacy Policy

We believe photography should be simple — and so should privacy. Here's what you need to know about how this app handles data.

1. No Data Collection

This app does not track you, profile you, or send your photos anywhere. Everything you take stays on your device, by design.

2. No Accounts or Sign-Ups

You don't need to create an account, sign in, or share personal information to use the app.

3. Permissions Explained

  • Camera — Needed so you can take photos. Nothing leaves your device unless you choose to share it.
  • Photo Library — Needed to save the photos you take, and optionally to pick existing photos. Your images stay local.
  • Location (optional) — Allows adding location info to your photos if you choose. We only use it when you allow it.

4. Standard App Analytics

We use Apple's built-in App Analytics through the App Store. This data is anonymous, does not identify you personally, and helps us improve the app.

5. No Hidden Business Model

The app is free to use, with no ads or in-app purchases. There are no hidden data monetization practices.

6. Night Photography

By design, this app does not use computational tricks. Low-light/night photos may not look as good as those from your standard iPhone camera. This is expected, and intentional, to keep photos authentic.

7. Contact

If you have questions, feedback, or notice something off, you can reach me at: your-email@example.com.

✅ Summary:

  • Your photos stay on your device.
  • We don't track you.
  • Permissions are only used for functionality you control.
  • The app is free, simple, and honest — just like the photos it helps you take.
